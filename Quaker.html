Quaker Parrot Diet

Let's look at what these parakeets eat!

Quaker Parrots, also known as Monk Parakeets, need to have a varied diet. Here are some other foods that Quakers like:

Rice (steamed only, no oil), Lettuce, Apple, Banana, Strawberries, Grapes, Melon, Pineapple, Berries, Oranges, Peaches, Pears, Peppers, Squash, Cooked Beans, Broccoli, Zucchini, Pumpkin, Peas, Leafy Greens, Sprouts, Cauliflower, and Celery.

Do not feed Quaker Parrots these foods:

Alcohol, Chocolate, or Avocado.

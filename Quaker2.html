Quacker Toys / Cage

Cages

The ideal cage for a Quacker Parrot should be at least 30 x 24 x 24 inches or larger.
The ideal bar spacing for Quaker parrots is between 5/8 to 3/4 inches.

Toys

Quaker parrots like to play and love to be the center of attention. They enjoy bottle caps,
hard non-toxic chew toys, puzzles, and standard bird toys.

Warning!

In my experience, Quaker parrots are very naughty and love to destroy toys...
